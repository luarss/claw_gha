## Signal Scoring Methodology

The aggregator scores signals on:
1. **Conviction** (1-5): Strength of thesis
2. **Risk-Reward** (ratio): Upside vs downside
3. **Catalyst Quality** (1-5): Near-term catalysts present
4. **Data Freshness** (< 48h required)

**Composite Score** = Conviction × 2 + R/R + Catalyst Quality
