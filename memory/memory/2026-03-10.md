### Investment Thesis Summary

**Amazon is a quality compounder at a fair price.** The company has successfully navigated the post-pandemic normalization, returned to strong profitability ($77B net income), and maintains multiple growth engines (AWS, Advertising, E-commerce, International). 

The 30x P/E is reasonable for a company growing earnings at 30%+ with dominant market positions. Unlike NVDA (cyclical semi exposure) or AAPL (hardware concentration), AMZN has diversified revenue streams with different growth drivers.

**Key differentiator:** AWS provides operating leverage and high margins, while retail provides scale and cash flow. Advertising is the hidden gem - high-margin, fast-growing, underappreciated.

**Best for:** Medium-to-long term holders (2-5 years) who want quality growth exposure with less valuation risk than NVDA and more growth than AAPL.
